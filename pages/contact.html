The Process

What Happens After You Reach Out

We respect your time. Every inquiry follows a structured, transparent onboarding process so you always know what comes next.

01
Inquiry Review

A senior associate reviews your submission within one business day and confirms receipt with a brief acknowledgement email.

02
Discovery Call

A 30-minute confidential call with a managing partner to understand your capital structure, goals, and immediate advisory priorities.

03
Preliminary Assessment

We prepare a no-obligation preliminary assessment of your current portfolio structure and identify the highest-impact advisory opportunities.

04
Engagement Proposal

If there is a mutual fit, we present a formal engagement proposal with a clear scope, fee structure, and projected outcomes.

We operate on a transparent fee-only model. Ongoing wealth management engagements are billed as a percentage of assets under advisory (AUA), typically between 0.5% and 1.0% annually depending on complexity. Project-based mandates such as tax restructuring or M&A advisory are billed at a fixed project fee agreed in advance.

Privacy & Security

Your Privacy Is a Non-Negotiable

When you contact Stackly, you are sharing sensitive financial information. We treat that responsibility with the same rigour we apply to managing capital.

End-to-End Encrypted Communications

All form submissions, emails, and document transfers are encrypted in transit and at rest using AES-256 protocols.

Fiduciary Confidentiality Obligation

As a registered fiduciary, Stackly is legally bound to maintain strict confidentiality over all client and prospective client information.

Isolated Data Infrastructure

Client data is stored on access-controlled, isolated servers with multi-factor authentication and regular third-party security audits.

No Data Sharing — Ever

We do not sell, share, or reference client data with any third party for any purpose, including marketing, research, or benchmarking.
