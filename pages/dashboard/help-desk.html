Frequently Asked Questions

Navigate to the **Statements & Tax** subpage under the Investment Center menu. There you will find download buttons in PDF format for your quarterly portfolio evaluations and active tax rosters.

Portfolio rebalancing is done quarterly by default to ensure alignment with target allocations. In the event of large market swings, your advisor will reach out to conduct an ad-hoc rebalancing analysis.

For security and compliance reasons, bank account link updates must be verified via dual signature. Please open a support ticket under the "Compliance Roster" category to receive the digital document links.

Advisors are active on chat from Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM EST. Urgent tickets submitted outside of these hours will be handled by our on-call operations desk.
