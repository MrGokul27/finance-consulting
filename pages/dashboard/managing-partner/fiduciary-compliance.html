Legal Standards

Fiduciary Duty Code

As a registered investment advisor (RIA), Stackly is bound under the Investment Advisers Act of 1940 to maintain a strict fiduciary standard of care:

1. Duty of Loyalty

We place client interests above firm profits and fully disclose conflicts of interest.

2. Duty of Care

We perform deep due diligence to ensure investment selections match client objectives.
