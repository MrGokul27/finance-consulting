Key Milestones & Growth

How we evolved our advisory frameworks to protect client wealth across shifting financial landscapes.

2010

Founding of Stackly Capital

Stackly was established in New York by Sowat Ahsan with a focused mandate: to provide premium corporate treasury optimization strategies for growing middle-market enterprises.

2015

The Fiduciary Shift

To remove commissions-based conflicts of interest, Stackly transitioned to a pure fee-only Registered Investment Advisor (RIA) model, ensuring alignment with clients' portfolios.

2020

Crossing $1.5 Billion AUM

Expanding advisory capabilities to include family office trusts, tax loss harvesting protocols, and advanced cross-border corporate structures, crossing the $1.5 Billion assets milestone.

2025

Risk-Modeling Launch

Stackly integrated advanced proprietary algorithmic stress-testing tools, modeling 40 years of market anomalies to isolate capital portfolios from drawdowns.
